## BEFORE YOU START {#before-you-start}

### Critical Rules for a True 100%

- **DO NOT join Company of Champions** unless you want increased difficulty. It disables co-op summons and makes enemies harder. You can join/leave freely at the Victor's Stone in Majula via Sweet Shalquoir.
- **DO NOT kill friendly NPCs** — their quests are needed for achievements. Only exception: if specifically noted.
- **ALWAYS exhaust NPC dialogue** — talk until they repeat. Many questline flags, gestures, and shop upgrades require this.
- **Save Fragrant Branches of Yore** — there are limited branches. Prioritize: Rosabeth (Shaded Woods access), Straid (boss weapon trades), Lion Warrior in Shaded Woods (needed for Fang Key), Basilisk in Doors of Pharros, and the statue in Black Gulch (for Lucatiel's hidden bonfire path).
- **ILLUSORY WALLS: Press A/X to open** (Xbox: A, PlayStation: X, PC: interact key) — **striking does NOT work** in DS2 (unlike DS1). Walk up and press the interact button.
- **Lucatiel and Benhart questlines are the most missable** — read their full checklist in the NPC section before starting. They require meeting them at specific locations AND keeping them alive through boss fights.
- **Human Effigies are precious** — being human lets you summon NPCs. You can also burn an effigy at a bonfire to stop invasions for 30 minutes.
- **You need 2.5 playthroughs minimum for all spells** — NG++ Chancellor Wellager sells unique spells. Alternatively, rank up Blue Sentinels and Brotherhood of Blood via PvP.
- **DLC spells count for Master trophies** in SotFS.

### Recommended Starting Class/Gift

- **Class:** Knight or Sorcerer (Knight is the Fighting Cowboy standard)
- **Gift:** Healing Wares (most practical) or Petrified Something (trade at bird nest for random item)

## THINGS BETWIXT {#things-betwixt}

### Before the House
- Pick up **Rusted Coin** from hidden area beside fallen tree (gap in wall, turn right)

### Inside the House
- Create your character (name, class, gift, appearance)
- Talk to **Milibeth** and the **Old Firekeepers**
- Chest upstairs: **Human Effigy**
- Destroy cart outside side door: **Soul of a Lost Undead** + **Torch**
- Light the **bonfire**

### Exploration
- Hidden path below the bridge: **Small Smooth & Silky Stone**
- Kill the **Ogre** near the bridge: **Stone Ring** (very good ring, staggers enemies)
- Corpse nearby: **Gold Pine Resin**

### Tutorial Area
- Fog Gate 1: Get **Dagger** and **Lifegem**
- At the bird nest (Dyna & Tillo): Drop the **Small Smooth & Silky Stone** for a random item. Kick down the ladder
- Drop off ledge: **Soul of a Nameless Soldier**
- Fog Gate 2: Jump gap for **Amber Herbs**; go up ladder, drop down, open door: **Cracked Red Eye Orb**
- Fog Gate 3: Push down trees, go outside
- Kill the **two Ogres** out here
- **COFFIN** — Sleep in the coffin to change your character's sex (purely cosmetic, no confirmation prompt)
- Return to Milibeth: Get **Handmaid's Ladle**

### Path to Majula
- Between the boulders on the right: **Morning Star**, **Cleric's Sacred Chime**, **Binoculars**

## MAJULA (FIRST VISIT) {#majula-first-visit}

### Essential First Steps
- Light **bonfire**
- Talk to **Emerald Herald** (Shanalotte): Get **Estus Flask**, she's your level-up NPC
- Path up toward Shaded Woods: **Divine Blessing**; talk to **Benhart of Jugo** (exhaust dialogue — DO NOT KILL)
- Near bonfire: **Lifegems**, **Homeward Bones**
- Building left of bonfire: Kill Hollow inside, get **Lloyd's Talismans**

### NPCs & Shops
- **Saulden the Crestfallen** (by monument): Learn **Welcome** gesture, join **Way of Blue** covenant (optional but good — kills 10 invaders for rank 3)
  - Tip: Die 100 times off the cliff and talk to him for **Ring of Steel Protection** (or just wait for a better one later)
- **Blacksmith Lenigrast**: Talk to him (locked out). You need **Lenigrast's Key** from Melentia (1,000 souls)
- **Sweet Shalquoir** (cat in house): Buy **Silvercat Ring** (13,400 souls — reduces fall damage, useful for the Pit later)
- **Maughlin the Armorer**: Buy armor. Spend 16,000 souls total with him, then talk to him with 0 souls in pocket for **Aurous Set**

### Items
- Well near mansion: Hit rock for **Estus Flask Shard #1**
- Spiral staircase down near pit: **Crimson Parma** (chest)
- Victor's Stone (hill): **Homeward Bones** nearby
- Ladder beside Maughlin: **Titanite Shard** (chest)
- Mansion: Use **House Key** (from Cale) later to enter. Basement has the map room
- Path to Forest of Fallen Giants: Get **Rusted Coin** from chest in building, pull switch. Drop down for **Human Effigy**, jump across for **Homeward Bone** + **Soul of a Lost Undead**

## FOREST OF FALLEN GIANTS {#forest-of-fallen-giants}

### Crestfallen's Retreat Bonfire
- By the river: **Lifegem**, **Soul of a Lost Undead**
- Up ladders, clear area. Jump gap: **Shortsword**, **Soul of a Lost Undead**
- Main area: **Broken Straight Sword**, **Human Effigy**, **Lifegem**
- Kill **Heide Knight** (sits by tree): **Heide Knight Sword**

### Cardinal Tower Bonfire
- Through fog gate and up: **Buckler**, **Witching Urns**
- Rest at bonfire. Talk to **Merchant Hag Melentia**
  - Buy: **Lenigrast's Key** (1,000 souls — PRIORITY), **Pharros Lockstone** (4,000)
  - Spend 10,000 souls total with her: Get **Covetous Silver Serpent Ring +1**
  - Exhaust her dialogue → she moves to Majula later
- Upstairs: Break door. Chest: **Small Leather Shield**, **Repair Powder**. Behind cart: **Hand Axe**, **Lifegem**
- Other door (ambush): Chest with **Estus Flask Shard #2** + **Small White Sign Soapstone**
- Drop on branch: **Divine Blessing**
- Scaffolding: **Human Effigy**, **Soul of a Lost Undead**, **Torch**

### Key Items in Caves Below
- First cave: **Hollow Soldier Helm**
- Second cave (ambush from behind): **Soul of a Proud Knight**
- Past the Flame Salamander: **Fire Longsword** (chest — excellent early weapon)
- Go to Majula: Upgrade **Estus Flask**, open Lenigrast's shop, get **Short Bow** from chest behind him

### Back to Forest — Cardinal Tower
- Down ladder, across bridge: various souls
- Optional: Silvercat Ring lets you survive drop to Flame Salamander pit: **Hawk Ring**, **Firedrake Stone**, **Large Soul of a Proud Knight**, chest: **Flame Quartz Ring +1**, cave: **Cracked Red Eye Orbs**, **Rebel's Greatshield**

### The Pursuer (Optional Early Fight)
- Through fog gate, up the branch, bait firebomb hollow to break wall (shortcut)
- Up ladder on platform: **The Pursuer** appears (can use ballista to cheese him)
- Drops: **Ring of Blades**, **Soul of the Pursuer**

### Cale the Cartographer
- Cave accessible from scaffolding: Talk to **Cale** — get **House Key** for the Majula mansion
- Exhaust dialogue → he moves to Majula mansion basement (map room)

### Soldier's Rest Bonfire (use Soldier Key from Last Giant)
- Open the locked doors with the **Soldier Key**

### Boss: THE LAST GIANT
- Summon: **Pate** (if you did his trap correctly — talked to him before AND after the gate trap near ballista room), **Sellsword Luet**
- Drops: **Soul of the Last Giant**, **Soldier Key**
- The Soldier Key opens 3 doors: 1) Door to Pursuer boss, 2) Soldier's Rest bonfire door, 3) King's Gate for Memory of Vammar (need Ashen Mist Heart later)

### Boss: THE PURSUER (Boss Arena)
- Access via Soldier Key door or the early platform encounter
- After killing him, examine the bird nest behind the arena → carried to **Lost Bastille**
- Drops: **Soul of the Pursuer**

## THE LOST BASTILLE / BELFRY LUNA {#the-lost-bastille}

### Arrival Points
- Via bird nest from Pursuer → **Tower Apart bonfire**
- Via ship from No-Man's Wharf → **Exile Holding Cells bonfire**

### Key Items
- **Tower Apart**: Left room has chests with **Dull Ember** (VERY IMPORTANT — give to McDuff)
- **Covetous Silver Serpent Ring** from a cell
- **Estus Flask Shard #3**: In a chest near Straid's cell area
- **Fragrant Branch of Yore**: Multiple in the area
- **Bastille Key**: After Ruin Sentinels boss (opens McDuff's gate and other doors)
- **Bracing Knuckle Ring +1**: Chest in explosive barrel area
- **Soul Vessel**: Hidden path

### NPCs

**Lucatiel of Mirrah — Location 2 of 4**
- In the tower straight ahead from McDuff's Workshop bonfire (or via Tower Apart)
- Lean against the wall in the tower. Exhaust dialogue
- Can be summoned for Lost Sinner (sign at bottom of elevator in Sinner's Rise)

**Straid of Olaphis**
- Petrified in a cell near Exile Holding Cells bonfire
- Use **Fragrant Branch of Yore** to free him
- He trades boss souls for spells/weapons
- Exhaust dialogue → learn **Mock** gesture

**Steady Hand McDuff**
- Give him the **Dull Ember** from Tower Apart → he becomes an infusion blacksmith
- Can access him by: 1) Blow up wall with explosive barrel enemy, or 2) Use Bastille Key on his gate
- Spend 14,000 souls with him → he gives **Titanite Slab**

### Boss: RUIN SENTINELS (3 of them)
- Very tough early boss. Fight the first one on the platform, then drop down for 2 more
- Drops: **Ruin Sentinel Soul**

### Boss: LOST SINNER (reached from Sinner's Rise — listed later)

### Belfry Luna
- Accessed through a Pharros Lockstone wall in the Lost Bastille
- **Bell Keeper covenant** — join here (talk to the dwarf)
- Kill invading Bell Keepers (or be invaded) for Titanite. Kill 30 for **Hidden Weapon** sorcery
- Boss: **Belfry Gargoyles** (5 gargoyles)
- Drops: **Belfry Gargoyle Soul**, **Covetous Gold Serpent Ring +2** (rooftop after boss)

## EARTHEN PEAK {#earthen-peak}

### Critical Secret: BURN THE WINDMILL
- Before Mytha boss: light a torch and interact with the windmill base to burn it
- This **drains all poison** from the boss arena and lower areas
- If you don't do this, Mytha's boss fight is nearly impossible (she heals from the poison pool)

### Key Items
- **Pharros Lockstone** locations: Use one for a hidden room
- **Mirrah Shield** (chest above moving platform)
- **Spell Quartz Ring +1**

### NPCs

**Laddersmith Gilligan**
- Near the windmill. Pay him 2,000 souls → learn **Prostration** gesture
- Exhaust dialogue → he moves to Majula (sets up ladders at the Pit)

**Lucatiel of Mirrah — Location 3 of 4 (SotFS)**
- **SotFS Location**: After entering Earthen Peak, go DOWN toward the poison-filled water. Follow the passage to the right. She's in the area near where the Poisonbite Ring is
- Exhaust ALL dialogue

**Mild-Mannered Pate** (if you met him previously)
- Revisit for questline progression

### Boss: MYTHA, THE BANEFUL QUEEN
- BURN THE WINDMILL FIRST
- Summon: **Jester Thomas** (very strong, casts pyromancies)
- Drops: **Mytha Soul**, access to Iron Keep

## BLACK GULCH {#black-gulch}

### Critical Warning
- Poison spitting statues EVERYWHERE — break them as you go or you'll die fast
- Use a torch or fire arrows to clear the poison statues

### Key Items
- **Fragrant Branch of Yore** (on a corpse JUST BEFORE entering Black Gulch — SAVE THIS for the hidden bonfire path)
- **Shotel** (in cave)
- **Scraps of Life** hex (in a cave with the worm enemies)

### NPC: Lucatiel of Mirrah — Location 4 of 4
- **HIDDEN LOCATION**: From the first bonfire, go left out the door with a lit torch. Edge along the cliff until you see a platform to DROP DOWN to (near three poison statues clustered together). Drop down and find Lucatiel in the cave tunnel
- Exhaust ALL dialogue — THIS COMPLETES her dialogue chain

### Hidden Bonfire for Lucatiel Summon
- From the boss fog gate, hug the LEFT wall, follow it to the cliff edge, go left on a narrow path
- **Petrified statue blocks the path** — use Fragrant Branch of Yore
- Behind it: **Hidden Chamber bonfire** + Lucatiel's summon sign
- Summon her for **The Rotten** — this is the easiest boss to keep her alive for

### NPC: Darkdiver Grandahl — Location 1 of 3
- In a cave along the RIGHT-hand ledge of Black Gulch
- Talk to him at all 3 locations to join **Pilgrims of Dark** covenant

### Boss: THE ROTTEN
- In the pit below. Summon Lucatiel (hidden bonfire)
- Watch for his AoE dark attack. Stay behind him
- Drops: **Old Dead One Soul** (Great Soul — Lord Soul)
- Light the **Primal Bonfire**

## SHADED WOODS {#shaded-woods}

### Getting There
- From Majula, past where Benhart sits, there's a petrified statue (Rosabeth of Melfia)
- Use **Fragrant Branch of Yore** on her → she comes alive

### NPC: Rosabeth of Melfia
- After unpetrifying, give her any piece of clothing (she asks)
- Exhaust dialogue → learn **Joy** gesture
- She moves to Majula

### NPC: Benhart of Jugo
- Talk to him again here → exhaust dialogue to progress his questline
- After unpetrifying Rosabeth, return to him for more dialogue

### Shaded Ruins (Foggy Area)
- The fog section: enemies are invisible. Stick to walls
- **LEFT WALL**: Leads to **Vengarl's Head**
  - Exhaust dialogue → learn **Decapitate** gesture
  - He tells you about his body (found later in Brightstone Cove)

### Scorpioness Najka Path
- Through the ruins, past the lion warriors
- **Fang Key** (requires unpetrifying a specific lion warrior with a Branch)

### NPC: Manscorpion Tark
- Before Najka's boss area. **Equip Ring of Whispers** (buy from Sweet Shalquoir) to understand him
- Exhaust dialogue → learn **Warmup** gesture
- He can be summoned for Najka

### Boss: SCORPIONESS NAJKA
- Drops: **Scorpioness Najka Soul**
- After killing: return to Tark with Ring of Whispers equipped → get **Second Dragon Ring** + **Fragrant Branch of Yore**

### Fork: Three Paths from Shaded Woods
1. **Left** (foggy) → Vengarl's Head, items
2. **Straight** → Doors of Pharros / Brightstone Cove Tseldora
3. **Right** → Shrine of Winter → Drangleic Castle (need enough souls or 4 Lord Souls)

**Invisible Hollows** (SotFS only) appear in certain areas; they become visible when hit or when close. Use area attacks or listen for footsteps.

## NG+ AND NG++ REQUIREMENTS {#ng-plus}

### What Changes in NG+
- All enemies are stronger and some have new placements
- New red phantom NPC invaders in many areas
- Some items only appear in NG+ (new rings, etc.)
- Boss souls are needed again for different trades

### NG++ (Third Playthrough) — CRITICAL
- **Chancellor Wellager** in Drangleic Castle sells unique spells:
  - **Great Lightning Spear** (miracle)
  - **Wrath of the Gods** (miracle)
  - **Bountiful Sunlight** (miracle)
  - **Blinding Bolt** (miracle)
- These are REQUIRED for Master of Miracles unless you rank up **Blue Sentinels** to rank 3 and **Brotherhood of Blood** to rank 3 via PvP (500 kills each)
- You only need to reach Wellager (early in the game), so it's a partial playthrough

### What to Do Before Starting NG+
- Buy ALL available spells from vendors
- Finish ALL NPC questlines
- Complete ALL DLC areas
- Collect all gestures
- Trade all boss souls you need
- Grab any items you missed

## EASILY MISSABLE THINGS — QUICK REFERENCE {#easily-missable}

### Top 10 Most Commonly Missed Things

1. **Lucatiel at Earthen Peak (SotFS)** — She moved from the bonfire. Go DOWN to the poison water area
2. **Lucatiel at Black Gulch** — Hidden cliff drop, easy to walk past
3. **Burning the Windmill** at Earthen Peak — drains poison for Mytha fight
4. **Talking to Lucatiel at Aldia's Keep BEFORE she survived 3 bosses** — ruins her questline
5. **Benhart summoned for enough boss fights** — easy to forget to summon him
6. **Agdayne (Undead Crypt) with torch lit** — permanently aggros him, lose gesture + hexes
7. **Darkdiver Grandahl's 3 locations** — one is a hidden drop-through floor in Drangleic Castle
8. **Manscorpion Tark without Ring of Whispers** — can't get Warmup gesture without the ring
9. **Chancellor Wellager dialogue** — must exhaust for This One's Me gesture
10. **Fragrant Branch priority** — using them on wrong statues and running out

### Secondary Missables
- Gavlan's 3 locations (for him to reach Doors of Pharros)
- Pate's trap sequence (must go through the gate AFTER talking to him)
- Rosabeth needs clothing GIVEN to her
- Sweet Shalquoir only sells Flying Feline Boots after BOTH rat bosses dead
- Navlaan's quests (must talk while hollow through the barrier)
- DLC spells count for trophies in SotFS
- Loyce Knights (4 must be freed before Ivory King boss for best experience)
