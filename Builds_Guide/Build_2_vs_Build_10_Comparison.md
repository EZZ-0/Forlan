# Build 2 vs Build 10 — Detailed Comparison

**Inferno Reaper** vs **Dark Chaos Blade Assassin**

A metric-by-metric analysis of two distinct DS2 SotFS builds at SL 150.

---

## Executive Summary

| Aspect | Build 2: Inferno Reaper | Build 10: Dark Chaos Assassin |
|--------|-------------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Archetype** | STR + Pyromancy | DEX + Hex |
| **Primary Weapon** | Red Rust power stance (dual curved swords) | Chaos Blade (katana) |
| **Damage Focus** | Physical + Fire | Physical + Dark |
| **Complexity** | Medium | High |
| **Risk/Reward** | Moderate | High risk, high reward |

---

## 1. Stat Distribution

| Stat | Build 2 (Inferno Reaper) | Build 10 (Dark Chaos Assassin) | Winner | Why |
|------|--------------------------|-------------------------------|--------|-----|
| **VGR** | 20 | 25 | **Build 10** | Chaos Blade drains 50 HP per hit; extra VGR offsets self-damage and improves survivability. |
| **END** | 20 | 25 | **Build 10** | Katana combos and roll-heavy playstyle need more stamina. |
| **VIT** | 20 | 10 | **Build 2** | Power stance + BKGS require heavier load; Build 2 wears more armor. |
| **ATN** | 43 | 25 | **Build 2** | 43 ATN = 2× Forbidden Sun casts; Build 2 leans harder on ranged nukes. |
| **STR** | 35 | 12 | **Build 2** | Red Rust power stance needs 35 STR; Build 10 is DEX-focused. |
| **DEX** | 25 | 40 | **Build 10** | Chaos Blade scales with DEX; Build 10 maximizes counter damage. |
| **ADP** | 20 | 25 | **Build 10** | Katana playstyle relies on dodging; higher ADP = more i-frames. |
| **INT** | 10 | 20 | **Build 10** | Dark Weapon requires 20 INT; Build 2 only needs pyro minimum (10). |
| **FTH** | 10 | 20 | **Build 10** | Dark Weapon requires 20 FTH; Build 2 only needs pyro minimum (10). |

**Takeaway:** Build 2 is STR-heavy with minimal casting stats. Build 10 is DEX-heavy with mandatory 20/20 INT/FTH for hexes.

## 2. Damage Type & Element Coverage

| Metric | Build 2 | Build 10 | Winner | Why |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|-----|
| **Primary Element** | Fire | Dark | — | Different niches; fire weak vs many DLC bosses, dark weak vs Velstadt/Nashandra. |
| **Secondary** | Physical (Red Rust, BKGS) | Physical (Chaos Blade) | — | Both have strong physical. |
| **Ranged Options** | Forbidden Sun, Flame Swathe, Great Chaos Fireball | Dark Orb, Resonant Soul | **Build 2** | Forbidden Sun is one of the highest burst spells; pyro has more AoE. |
| **Boss Coverage** | Fire-resistant bosses (e.g. Smelter, Fume) can be tough | Dark-resistant bosses (Velstadt, Darklurker) need swap | — | Each has blind spots; Build 2 can fall back on BKGS physical. |

**Takeaway:** Build 2 has stronger ranged burst. Build 10 has Dark Weapon for melee buff but fewer ranged tools. Both struggle vs their element-resistant bosses.

## 3. Playstyle & Complexity

| Metric | Build 2 | Build 10 | Winner | Why |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|-----|
| **Melee Style** | Power stance dual curved swords; heavy, sweeping attacks | Single katana; fast, counter-focused | — | Subjective; power stance = more commitment, katana = hit-and-run. |
| **Spell Management** | Pyromancy (no stat scaling beyond 10/10) | Hexes (20/20 required; Resonant Soul costs souls) | **Build 2** | Pyro is simpler; hexes add soul-cost and stat pressure. |
| **Resource Management** | Estus, spell uses | Estus, lifegems (Chaos Blade drain), souls (Resonant Soul) | **Build 2** | Build 10 must stock lifegems and manage soul economy. |
| **Execution** | Medium — power stance timing, spell swaps | High — Chaos Blade drain, buff uptime, dodging | **Build 2** | Build 10 punishes mistakes more. |

**Takeaway:** Build 2 is easier to pilot. Build 10 demands tighter play and resource awareness.

## 4. Accessibility & Route

| Metric | Build 2 | Build 10 | Winner | Why |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|-----|
| **70% Complete** | Doors of Pharros (Red Rust), Shaded Woods (Pyro Flame) | No-Man's Wharf (Flexile soul), Huntsman's (Felkin 20/20) | **Build 10** | Chaos Blade + Dark Weapon online earlier; Red Rust needs Vengarl at Doors of Pharros. |
| **100% Complete** | Iron Keep (BKGS farm), Aldia's (Forbidden Sun), NG+ rings | Huntsman's (Felkin), Lost Bastille (McDuff), NG+ rings | **Build 10** | Build 10 avoids BKGS farm; Build 2 needs rare drop + NG+ for RoB+2. |
| **Key Bottlenecks** | Black Knight Greatsword (rare drop), Southern Ritual +2 (NG+ Najka) | 20/20 base INT/FTH (Felkin), Chaos Blade (boss soul) | **Build 2** | BKGS farm is tedious; Build 10's 20/20 is a stat choice, not RNG. |
| **Starting Class** | Deprived (SL 1, blank slate) | Bandit (DEX/ADP start) | **Build 10** | Bandit aligns with DEX; Deprived requires more early leveling. |

**Takeaway:** Build 10 comes online sooner and has fewer RNG gates. Build 2 is more grind-heavy for full completion.

## 5. Survivability

| Metric | Build 2 | Build 10 | Winner | Why |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|-----|
| **HP Pool** | 20 VGR (~900 HP) | 25 VGR (~1,100 HP) | **Build 10** | Higher VGR compensates for Chaos Blade drain. |
| **Armor** | VIT 20 — medium/heavy possible | VIT 10 — light armor only | **Build 2** | Build 2 can tank more; Build 10 relies on dodging. |
| **Self-Damage** | None | 50 HP per Chaos Blade hit | **Build 2** | Build 10 actively hurts itself; lifegems mandatory. |
| **Healing** | Estus | Estus + lifegems | **Build 10** | Build 10 needs more healing items due to drain. |
| **Dodge Reliance** | Moderate | High | — | Build 10 must dodge; Build 2 can absorb some hits. |

**Takeaway:** Build 2 is tankier. Build 10 has more HP but bleeds it; survivability depends on skill and lifegem management.

## 6. Burst Damage

| Metric | Build 2 | Build 10 | Winner | Why |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|-----|
| **Melee Burst** | Power stance L1 combos, BKGS 2H | Chaos Blade + Dark Weapon counter hits | **Build 10** | Chaos Blade has 150 counter; Dark Weapon adds huge dark AR. |
| **Ranged Burst** | Forbidden Sun (2 casts at ATN 43), Flame Swathe | Resonant Soul (costs souls), Dark Orb | **Build 2** | Forbidden Sun is top-tier nuke; pyro burst > hex burst at same investment. |
| **Boss Nuke** | Flame Swathe ×3 (9 casts), Forbidden Sun | Dark Weapon + Chaos Blade R1 spam | **Build 2** | Stacked Flame Swathe melts bosses; Build 10 is sustained melee. |

**Takeaway:** Build 2 wins ranged burst. Build 10 wins melee burst (counter + buff). For boss nuking from range, Build 2 is superior.

| Metric | Build 2 | Build 10 | Winner | Why |

## 10. Recommendation

- **Choose Build 2 (Inferno Reaper)** if you want:
  - A STR-focused hybrid with strong ranged options
  - Easier resource management (no self-damage, no soul-cost spells)
  - Better PvE and DLC performance
  - More forgiving playstyle

- **Choose Build 10 (Dark Chaos Assassin)** if you want:
  - Maximum melee burst and counter damage
  - A high-skill, high-reward katana build
  - PvP-oriented play
  - Faster route to 70% build completion (fewer grinds)
